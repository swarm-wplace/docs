Welcome! Its great you want to join us on planka, and we welcome you!

<Steps>

 1. 
    Firstly, open the [Planka site](https://swplace2.nagol12344.tech) if you don't have it open already.
 2. 
    Please click on "Sign In with Discord" under the login section.
    <img src="/account-creation/loginWithDiscordButton.png"></img>
 3.
    You will be redirected to Discords site, where you will have to authorize planka to use your discord account.
    <img src="/account-creation/discordAuthorizationPage.png"></img>
 4.
    After authorizing, you will be redirected back to planka, where your will have to confirm the username, and then create your account.
    Please make sure you agree with the username and name Planka gives you, as changing it later is annoying.
    <img src="/account-creation/ConfirmAccountCreate.png"></img>
 5.
    Agree to the Terms of Service and your done! Enjoy using Planka and good luck with your pixeling!
    <Aside variant="info">
      If you get the "Unknown error, try again later" error or another error, please ping Nagol12344 on Discord!
    </Aside>


</Steps>
